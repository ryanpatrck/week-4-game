Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be give a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.You lost the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
